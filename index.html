About

        Vithai-17 Foundation was founded on July 1, 2023, under the visionary leadership of P S Senthil Srinivas, a pre-final year student pursuing a BE in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Sri Sairam Engineering College. Inspired by the "Omega Give Away Campaign" during his time at Lalaji Memorial Omega International School and an alumni talk by Leo Akask Raj, Senthil's vision has always been directed towards social work and contributing to a better society.

        After six years of perseverance and dedication, Senthil sowed the seeds of sustainability through Vithai-17 Foundation with his school friends, M Vishal and K Praveen Ramanan. What started as a small initiative has grown into a thriving community of 25 dedicated members. Together, the team has positively impacted over 150 individuals through various initiatives.

        One of the notable collaborations of Vithai-17 Foundation is with "Your House Our House," an old age home, starting from December 2023. The foundation has extended its efforts to the educational sector with the launch of its first initiative, "Speak UP-1.0," where Ms. Bonny Roy trained 10 students to communicate effectively in English, helping them break barriers in their career starts.

        At Vithai-17 Foundation, our mission is to create a sustainable and socially responsible society by empowering individuals and communities through education, support, and collaboration.
